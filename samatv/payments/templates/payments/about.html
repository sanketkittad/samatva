Established in the year 2002 in Pune, we aim to provide quality education to all children, and provide equal opportunities for children from less fortunate backgrounds as others.

"समत्व" means equality in Hindi. Our goal is to provide each individual an equal opportunity to prove his/her worth.

Expanded into 20 cities by 2008. Over 30 thousand children benefited.

Started the Volunteering program in 2010. Our volunteers provide Educational classes, Mentoring, Career guidance, etc. to less fortunate children.

Having presence in over 50 cities today. Over 1 Lac children benefited from our organisation. Over 9 Lac ₹ raised and counting...
